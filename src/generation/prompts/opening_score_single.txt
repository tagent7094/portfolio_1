1. stop_scroll (1-10)
   Would this make someone physically stop their thumb mid-scroll?
   10 = involuntary stop. 5 = might pause. 1 = keep scrolling without registering it.

2. specificity (1-10)
   Does it contain a concrete, non-generic detail?
   10 = a number, quote, or moment too specific to be invented.
   1 = could apply to any post by any person on any topic.

3. humanness (1-10)
   Does this sound like a person or like a content template?
   10 = clearly typed by a specific human with a specific experience.
   1 = AI-generated, polished, interchangeable.

4. curiosity (1-10)
   Does the reader NEED to know what comes next?
   10 = the reader cannot not keep reading.
   5 = mild interest.
   1 = the reader already knows where this is going.

5. body_fit (1-10)
   Does this opening honestly set up the post that follows?
   10 = perfect tonal and topical bridge into the body.
   1 = bait-and-switch — the opening promises something the body does not deliver.
