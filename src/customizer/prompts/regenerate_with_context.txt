UNIVERSAL ANTI-PATTERN GUARD
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Before outputting, scan for and remove:
- "Not X. Y." / "It's not about A. It's about B." appearing twice or more in sequence
- Three-word fragment chains in sequence (staccato AI giveaway)
- Em-dashes used for dramatic pause
- Rhetorical questions as transitions ("So what changed?" / "But what does that mean?")
- "Let me be clear" / "Here's the thing" / "The truth is"
- "I realized something profound" — the reader decides
- "I know this might be controversial..."
- Performing authority with tone instead of earning it with specifics
- Perfect symmetry in contrasts — real speech is asymmetric
- Every section ending with a clean quotable one-liner
