## YOUR TASK

### 1. NARRATIVE ARC
Identify the **narrative arc** — what movement does this post run on? (e.g., "contradiction reveal", "progressive escalation", "scene-to-insight pivot", "confession-to-principle")

### 2. HOOK MECHANICS
Break the opening hook into its **mechanical parts**. For EACH of the opening sentences (typically 1-3 lines before the body begins):
- What is this sentence DOING structurally? (e.g., "permission frame", "diagnostic question", "consequence statement", "reframe", "scene drop", "specificity bomb", "tension gap")
- What is its rhythm? (short/punchy, medium/flowing, long/building)
- How does it connect to the next sentence?

### 3. SENTENCE MAP
Map the **exact sentence count and structural function** of the opening lines. This is the mold that will be mirrored.

### 4. BODY STRUCTURE
How is the body organized? (e.g., "single scene explored deeply", "three examples building", "before/after transformation", "progressive revelation")

### 5. ENDING TYPE
What kind of ending is it? (e.g., "concrete image", "challenge to reader", "quiet admission", "unresolved tension", "physical detail")

### 6. WHY IT WENT VIRAL
In one sentence: what makes the OPENING of this post work for a GENERAL audience (not just domain experts)?
