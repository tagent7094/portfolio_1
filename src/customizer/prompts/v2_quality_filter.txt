## 11-POINT QUALITY FILTER

Evaluate each check. Be honest and specific. A post that fails more than 1 check should be flagged.

1. **MACRO TEST** — Does the opening hook pass the macro test? Would a GENERAL audience (not just domain experts) relate to it and feel curiosity? Or is it too niche / too intellectual?

2. **VULNERABILITY** — Does the body contain at least one moment where the founder is vulnerable/exposed, not lecturing?

3. **COHESIVE NARRATIVE** — Does the body read as ONE cohesive narrative? Or does it feel like assembled content pieces stitched together?

4. **ENDING** — Does the ending avoid restating the argument and land on a concrete detail (image, name, challenge, admission, physical object)?

5. **PHYSICAL SCENE** — Could you picture a specific physical scene in this post? A moment with a setting, a person, a detail?

6. **FOUNDER INSIDE** — Is the founder INSIDE the experience, or observing from outside?

7. **HOOK DELIVERY** — Does the body deliver on the hook's SPECIFIC promise? Or did it drift to an adjacent topic?

8. **EVENT FRESHNESS** — Is every specific event/number/milestone used only once across all variants? Check against the events list above.

9. **NO RECYCLED LINES** — Would any sentence read as copy-pasted from one of the founder's existing published posts? If so, flag it.

10. **WORD COUNT** — Is the word count within the founder's typical range ({word_count_range})?

11. **DINNER TABLE TEST** — Read it out loud mentally. Does it sound like a person talking, or like AI-assembled content?
