PASS 0: STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY (RUN BEFORE ANYTHING ELSE)

These checks catch framework violations that survived generation:

- HOOK-PROMISE CHECK: Restate the hook's promise in one sentence. Does every body paragraph advance THAT argument? If any paragraph drifts to an adjacent topic, cut or restructure it.
- ONE-ARRIVAL CHECK: Count standalone quotable lines. If more than one, keep the sharpest (usually the last), fold or delete the others.
- VISIBLE-MOMENT CHECK: Is there at least one scene you can picture? Is the founder inside it? If the post only analyzes patterns without personal stakes, inject a scene from the founder's world or flag for rewrite.
- VULNERABILITY CHECK: Is the founder exposed/not in control in at least one moment? Or is the entire post lecturing? If lecturing only, soften one moment toward vulnerability.
- RECYCLING CHECK: Could any sentence be copy-pasted from the founder's published posts? If yes, rewrite until it can't.
- WORD-COUNT CHECK: Is the post within the founder's typical range? If significantly over, cut.
- THROAT-CLEARING CHECK: Could the first sentence be deleted without losing meaning? If yes, delete it.

PASS 1: KILL AI STRUCTURES
- Collapse parallel triplets to 2 or break with fragment
- Cut "while X, Y", "on the other hand", "that said"
- Scramble neat 1-2-3 escalation. State conclusion, back into evidence.
- Break uniform paragraph length. Mix long/short/fragment.

PASS 2: STRIP VOCABULARY TELLS
- Heavy English → simplest word (utilize→use, facilitate→help)
- BANNED ZERO: landscape, navigate, leverage, foster, harness, unlock, delve, synergy, robust, nuanced, revolutionary, game-changing, seamless, empower, holistic, paradigm shift, move the needle, "Here's what people miss", "Here's the thing", "The truth is", "Let that sink in", "In today's [X]", "Real talk", "Hard truth", "Nobody talks about"
- Fake emotion → show it. "I'm excited" → just share it.
- Hedges → assert. "This can sometimes" → "This usually means" or drop.

PASS 3: NEUTRALIZE SPECIAL CHARACTERS
- Em dashes → period or line break
- Semicolons in casual prose → split sentences
- >2 bold phrases → strip all but 1
- Ellipsis >1 → cut
- Mid-sentence CAPS → remove

PASS 4: ELIMINATE REPETITION
- 4+ word phrase used twice → rewrite one
- Sentence opener used 3+ times → vary
- Same point twice → keep sharper, delete other
- LinkedIn clichés → replace with founder-specific truth

PASS 5: INJECT HUMAN RHYTHM
- Target: long. short. medium. fragment. (1-3 fragments/post)
- Default to contractions unless founder is formally rigid
- Add ONE human touch: mid-thought pivot, self-correction, casual parenthetical, or unfinished qualification. Place where tension peaks.

PASS 6: FOUNDER LOCK
- Every sentence: "Would THIS founder type this exactly?"
- At least ONE sentence only they could write (industry position, public belief, personal idiom)
- Peer-generic → rewrite or cut.
