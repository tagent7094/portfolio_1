## THE 13 PROVEN OPENER MECHANICS
1. Specific number + context — "$412K closed last quarter. Target was $350K."
2. Juxtaposition — "Our competitor raised $40M. We outshipped them with 3 engineers."
3. First-person confession — "I told 50 buyers we were ready. I was wrong."
4. Quote hook + reaction — "'We need you to present to the supervisor.' That's when I knew the deal was dead."
5. Scene-entry — "We walked into their call center. Post-it notes on every monitor."
6. Decision reveal — "A Fortune 500 asked us to build a CRM. We said no."
7. Contrarian declaration — "Most founder advice is wrong."
8. Pattern observation — "Every founder I know who's crossed $20M runs into the same wall."
9. Tension pair — "The market says automate everything. Customers keep asking for humans."
10. Before/after snapshot — "Two years ago we couldn't close a single deal. Last month we turned down three."
11. Micro story — "An investor asked what happens if a competitor copies us. I showed him our NPS scores."
12. Authority redirect — "McKinsey says 70% of transformations fail. They left out why the 30% succeed."
13. Data inversion — "95% customer satisfaction. We were measuring the wrong customers."

## RULES FOR ALL POSTS

1. **MANDATORY EVENT USAGE**: Every post MUST include at least one verified first-degree authority anchor from the FOUNDER INTERNALIZATION (signature scenes, key moments, story bank). A post with zero personal evidence reads as generic advice, not earned authority.

3. **ANTI-RECYCLING**: If a sentence could be copy-pasted from the founder's published posts, rewrite it. Treat their existing posts as raw material, not finished material.

4. **VOICE COMPLIANCE (CRITICAL — post will be voice-validated after generation)**: The post MUST pass 5-dimension voice validation on first try.
