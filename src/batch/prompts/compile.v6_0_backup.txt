## YOUR TASK

Review the validated pack and make a final ship or reject decision.

### SHIP CRITERIA
- At least 7 of 9 posts pass the 9/7 floor (every parameter ≥ 7, at least one ≥ 9)
- No post has a critical voice violation (register reads as corporate/generic)
- Anchor diversity: at least 5 different anchors used across the pack
- No two posts share the same argument_compressed thesis
- Mechanic distribution: at least 4 different opener mechanics across the pack

### REJECT CRITERIA
- More than 2 posts fail the 9/7 floor
- Any post reads as corporate/press-release
- Anchor concentration: same anchor used 3+ times
- Argument convergence: 2+ posts argue the same thesis
