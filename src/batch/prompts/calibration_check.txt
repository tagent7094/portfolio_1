## TASK

Draft ONE opening paragraph (2-3 sentences) on this neutral topic: "What most people get wrong about hiring."

This is a calibration test. The paragraph must:
1. Sound like THIS specific founder wrote it — not "a CEO" or "a thought leader" or "a generic LinkedIn poster"
2. Match their sentence rhythm, word choices, and formatting habits EXACTLY
3. Fall within their typical word count density
4. Use at least 2 of their voice markers naturally
5. Match their formatting: if they use em dashes, use em dashes. If they tag team members, end with tags. If they use ":)", use ":)". Formatting mismatch is the biggest AI tell.
6. Avoid every pattern on the anti-slop list:
   - No "In today's..."
   - No "Here's the thing"
   - No "Hot take:" / "Unpopular opinion:"
   - No "Let that sink in" / "Read that again"
   - No "Game-changer" / "Landscape" / "Navigate" / "Leverage"

The recognition test: would this founder read the paragraph and think "this is what I was going to say"? If they'd have to rewrite more than one sentence, the voice work failed.
