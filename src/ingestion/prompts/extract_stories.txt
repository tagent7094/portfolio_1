You are extracting STORIES — specific events, anecdotes, and lived experiences this person tells. Stories are the credibility anchors that make content feel EARNED, not theorized.

## WHAT COUNTS AS A STORY
A story requires ALL three:
1. A **specific event** at an identifiable moment (not a general observation)
2. A **protagonist** (usually the person, sometimes a colleague, customer, or competitor)
3. A **change of state** (lesson learned, decision made, assumption broken, outcome revealed)

## WHAT IS NOT A STORY (do NOT extract)
❌ "AI is augmenting, not replacing" — that's a BELIEF (no event, no protagonist)
❌ "I've seen this pattern in 50 companies" — that's a PATTERN OBSERVATION (no specific event)
❌ "Revenue grew 3x" — that's a DATA POINT (no narrative arc)
❌ "Uses short sentences" — that's a STYLE RULE
❌ General observations, statistics, or opinions without a SPECIFIC EVENT
