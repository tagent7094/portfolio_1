## WHAT COUNTS AS A BELIEF
A belief is something this person treats as TRUE and would argue for:
- Prescriptive: "You should..." / "Stop doing X, start doing Y"
- Declarative conviction: "X is broken" / "Y doesn't work the way people think"
- Contrarian position: Goes against mainstream consensus WITH reasoning
- Repeated theme: Shows up across multiple posts/contexts
