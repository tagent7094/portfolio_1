## MANDATORY OUTLINE STRUCTURE

### 1. TITLE (H1) — REQUIRED CRITERIA
- 50-65 characters
- Contains primary_keyword in first 60% of title
- Hooks emotionally using the founder's voice
- NOT a question (questions belong in H2s)

### 2. URL SLUG — REQUIRED CRITERIA
- 3-5 words
- Contains primary_keyword
- Hyphenated, lowercase

### 3. META DESCRIPTION — REQUIRED CRITERIA
- 140-155 characters (will be truncated above this in Google)
- Contains primary_keyword once
- Reads like a human wrote it, not a template
- Ends with curiosity hook or value promise

### 4. INTRO HOOK (no heading, 100-150 words) — REQUIRED CRITERIA
- Sentence 1: contains primary_keyword naturally
- Sentences 2-4: stake the founder's specific take using founder voice
- Last sentence: tension or question the body resolves
- Uses at least 1 founder voice marker

### 5. BODY: 5-8 H2 SECTIONS — REQUIRED CRITERIA
- MINIMUM 5 H2 headings (this is non-negotiable)
- At least 2 H2 headings MUST be drawn from people_also_ask_targets (will rank in PAA panels)
- Other H2s should incorporate long_tail_variations or related_entities naturally
- Each H2 section: 250-450 words
- Each section advances ONE distinct sub-argument (no repetition between sections)
- Each section must reference at least 1 related_entity from the SEO inputs
- At least 50% of sections must surface a verified founder belief or story

### 6. STRUCTURED ELEMENTS — MINIMUM 3 OF THESE 5 REQUIRED
- Bulleted or numbered list inside one section (drives featured snippets)
- Comparison frame ("X vs Y") for featured snippet eligibility
- Definition box for a key term in the topic
- Numbered step-by-step (if how_to format)
- 4-6 question FAQ section (mandatory if search_intent = informational)

### 7. FAQ SECTION (if informational intent — mandatory)
- 4-6 questions drawn from people_also_ask_targets and searchable_questions
- Each answer: 50-100 words
- Each question is an H3 heading (FAQ schema-ready)

### 8. CONCLUSION (H2: typically "What this means for [audience]")
- Ties back to founder's core conviction
- Includes specific CTA (read another post / try X / consider Y)
- Uses founder voice marker (e.g., "Ask yourself..." closer)

### 9. INTERNAL LINK PLACEHOLDERS
- Identify 3-5 phrases where internal links to other founder content would fit
- Mark with {{{{LINK: anchor text}}}} placeholders

### 10. KEYWORD DENSITY TARGETS
- Primary keyword appears 4-8 times in body (NOT keyword-stuffed)
- Each related entity appears at least once
- Long-tail variations: at least 3 of them appear in body
