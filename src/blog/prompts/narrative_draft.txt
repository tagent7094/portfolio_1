## FORMAT-SPECIFIC STRUCTURE

### If thought_leadership:
- Bold opening statement (the founder's thesis)
- 3-4 supporting arguments with evidence from transcript + experience
- Contrarian element: where they disagree with conventional wisdom
- Closing conviction statement

### If behind_the_scenes:
- Scene-setting opener (a specific moment from the transcript)
- The real story behind a decision/challenge/breakthrough
- Candid reflection on what they learned
- Takeaway for the reader

### If listicle:
- Hook that establishes credibility on this topic
- 5-8 numbered items, each with a clear headline + 100-200 word explanation
- Closing that ties the list together

### If how_to:
- Problem statement the reader relates to
- Step-by-step framework (3-7 steps)
- Each step includes founder's personal experience or example
- Common mistakes to avoid

## RULES
1. Use actual quotes from the transcript as anchors — but rewrite surrounding context in the founder's blog voice
2. The recognition test: would the founder read this and think "this captures what I meant"?
3. No corporate language. No AI slop. No "in today's landscape."
4. Blog voice = slightly more expansive than LinkedIn, but same register
5. Hit the word target (±15%)
