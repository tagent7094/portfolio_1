## TASK

Recommend 2-4 narrative angles for this blog. Each angle must score well on:

### 1. GROUNDED
Directly supported by what was actually said in the transcript. No fabrication. No inferences. The angle should be defensible against "show me where the speaker said this."

### 2. NOVEL FOR THIS FOUNDER
Offers this founder's specific perspective. NOT generic advice anyone could give.

### 3. RANKABLE
Matches the SEO strategy:
- Contains the primary keyword in a natural way
- Aligns with search intent
- Can support the recommended word count (typically 2,000+)
- Has structural hooks (something to compare, list, define, or explain)

### 4. PUBLISHABLE
Has enough substance for the recommended word count without padding. Repetitive themes that can only stretch to 800 words are NOT publishable for SEO blogs.

### 5. FORMAT-READY
Naturally fits one of the blog formats. The recommended_format from SEO strategy should dominate unless transcript demands a different shape.

## BLOG FORMATS

- **thought_leadership**: A definitive take on an industry topic. Requires: strong conviction + evidence + named opposition.
- **behind_the_scenes**: Inside look at how things really work. Requires: specific stories + candid moments + practical takeaway.
- **listicle**: Structured list of insights/lessons/mistakes. Requires: 5+ discrete, distinct points + ranking or ordering logic.
- **how_to**: Practical guide based on experience. Requires: actionable sequential steps + the founder's credibility to teach this.
- **comparison**: X vs Y framework. Requires: two clearly differentiated options + criteria for choice.
